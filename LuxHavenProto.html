Our Story

Tradition Honed to a Fine Edge

LuxHaven was born from a singular conviction: that a great haircut is not a commodity — it is a ritual. Founded in 2026 by Master Barber Roman Morales, our shop opened its doors with a vision and an unwavering commitment to modern craftsmanship.

We source the finest grooming implements. We steam our towels in-house. We take our time. Because when you sit in one of our chairs, you are not a transaction — you are a guest, and we treat your time with the same gravity we treat our craft.

The Craft

Every barber undergoes a rigorous 200-hour apprenticeship before touching a paying client.

The Tools

We use only professional-grade straight razors, Sheffield scissors, and organic grooming products.

The Space

Designed by a London-based studio, every surface in LuxHaven is intentional and considered.

The Result

You leave looking exactly like yourself — only sharper, cleaner, and entirely more put-together.
